Our Story

Founded in 2015, Spicy Symphony brings together the best culinary traditions from India, Italy, and beyond. Our chef, Rajiv Kapoor, trained in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe before returning to his roots to create this unique fusion experience.

We believe in "no compromise" - authentic spices imported directly from their regions of origin, locally-sourced organic produce, and traditional cooking methods adapted for modern palates.

Chef Rajiv Kapoor
